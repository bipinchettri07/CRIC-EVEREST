How to Play

Welcome to the Super Over game! Follow these steps to play:

  1. Choose the number of runs you want to score by clicking on the buttons labeled with numbers.
  2. After each click, the computer will randomly choose the number of runs it scores.
  3. The game keeps track of the total runs scored by you and the computer.
  4. Try to score more runs than the computer within the given number of balls.
  5. Once Both Number matches, the game ends for a player and shift to another, and the winner is determined.

Are you ready to play? Click the button below to start!
